Project Overview

Golden Eye is an autonomous, AI-powered drone system designed for search and rescue operations in disaster-hit or inaccessible regions. The project integrates computer vision, real-time object detection, and GPS-based tracking to identify and locate individuals in need of help. Equipped with onboard intelligence, Golden Eye can autonomously navigate through complex environments, transmit live video feeds, and deploy small payloads, such as emergency supplies or communication devices. The system leverages OpenCV, TensorFlow, and DroneKit, and operates via an ESP32-based control architecture. This project aims to assist first responders by reducing human risk, accelerating victim detection, and enhancing the efficiency of emergency operations..

Technologies Used

  • AI & Computer Vision: TensorFlow for deep learning, OpenCV for image processing and camera integration.
  • Navigation & Control: DroneKit for autonomous flight, Neo-6M GPS module for live tracking, ESP32 for communication control.
  • Communication & Streaming: ESP-NOW/Wi-Fi for wireless data transmission, camera module for live video feed.
  • Hardware Integration: Brushless DC motors and ESCs for propulsion, Li-Po battery for power, custom payload mechanism for deliveries.
